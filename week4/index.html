Exciting Expeditions

We run rivers!

The Snake River offers both calm, relaxing waters with stunning scenery to wild rapids for thrill seekers. Exciting Expeditions provides everything you need to enjoy what the river has to offer. We have a tours for everyone.

We run rivers to relax

Start your journey down the Snake River at Hobble Cove. The river at this point and for the next few miles is calm and relaxing. Rafters of all ages enjoy the scenery, fishing, and an occasional dip. It's a great opportunity to cool off.

We run whitewater for the rush

For those wanting a more exciting adventure, Wild Rock Canyon offers thrills with its class III-IV rapids.

Come run with us!

Whether you're looking for a relaxing afternoon on the river or an exciting adventure, the Snake River and Exciting Expeditions is your one-stop for fun! We offer rentals and tours ranging from three hours to one week. Call us and book your tour
