Site Purpose

This is the website for Exciting Expeditions, a river rafting company offering summer rafting tours. It will include information about the company, hours of operation, services offered, prices, and contact information.

Audience

Exciting Expeditions offers several options for those seeking adventure. Call for group rates.

  • Individual adventure seekers
  • Couples
  • Families (adult supervision of children under 14 required)
  • Youth Groups
