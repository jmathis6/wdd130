2021 News

Jaxon & Shaylie both win their first basketball games of the season!

Saturday, December 4, 2021. Jaxon and Shaylie played their first basketball games of the season. Jaxon made two three point shots and two layups for a total of 10 of his teams' 22 points for the win! While Shaylie didn't make any baskets this game, she had several assists and helped her team toward their victory. Congratulations to our star athletes!

Olivia Shines at Dance Recital

December 1, 2021. Olivia performed beautifully in her Christmas dance recital held at the Payson High School. Olivia is a true showgirl; she had the biggest smile on stage!

Julie Discovers a New Restaurant with Friends

December 6, 2021. Julie traveled to Richfield with neighbors, Cheryl Levi, and Beth Spotten, to the Memorial Service of their old friend, Mark Berthelson who passed away from a car accident on November 17th. After the service, the women discovered a new restaurant, R & R Frontier Village, in Richfield. The food was good and the company even better. It just happened to be Beth's 72nd birthday and the women celebrated by sharing a chocolate lava cake.

The three women have been friends for 25 years. Together with Janice Berthelson (Mark's wife), the women taught the neighborhood children 4-H and served together in the cub scouts.
