Site Name

Mathis Family Archives

Site Purpose

This is the website for the Gordon Mathis family to share the following:

Calendar & Upcoming Events

This page will either link to a Google calendar or provide a list of family birthdays, upcoming events, and holidays.

News

The newsletter will include news family members want to share. This may include pictures.

Family Dinner

This page will provide our family dinner schedule for the year.

Recipes

Family members can share their favorite recipes on this page.

Pictures

This will be an online family photo album.

Memories

This page will provide a way for family members to share favorite memories from the past that will become a part of our family history.

Family History

This page will have stories and pictures of ancestors and link to FamilySearch.

Audience

Members of the Gordon Mathis family.
