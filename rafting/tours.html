Half-day Tour

Run is 5 miles and takes approximately 2-3 hours (from check-in to finish).

Features Class I and Class II water with light riffles, clear passages, gentle curves, and stunning scenery.

Perfect for

  • Relaxing and enjoying a sunny afternoon
  • Stopping occasionally to swim, fish, or have a picnic on the bank
  • Beginners learning to handle a rented tube or raft (yes, we rent these!)
  • Families (minimum age: 4 with adult supervision)
  • Youth groups and family reunions

Full-day Tour

Run is 10 miles and takes approximately 6-7 hours (from check-in to finish).

Begins with a gentle run where you can relax and enjoy stunning scenery, and then moves into exciting Class III rapids to get your heart pumping.

Perfect for

  • Intermediate and experienced rafters (self-guided tour)
  • Individuals and groups led by an experienced tour guide
  • Anyone looking for a combination of relaxation and thrills
  • Nature lovers and enthusiasts
  • Minimum age: 12-13 with adult supervision

Guided Tour

$100/Adult (10+)

Includes raft, paddles, life jackets, helmet (opt.), and shuttle service

Full-week Tour

Six days on the river (camp on the river bank).

Includes relaxing stretches of river with beautiful scenery you can’t see any other way and Class III and Class IV rapids that provide the thrill and excitement adventurers crave. We provide all meals. Camping gear and wet bag rental available.

Perfect for

  • Experienced rafters with advanced training (self-guided tours)
  • Tour groups with an experienced tour guide with advanced training
  • Adventure seekers
  • Nature lovers and enthusiasts
  • Minimum age: 14 with adult supervision

Guided Tour

$1000/Adult (12+)

Includes tour guide, raft, paddles, life jackets, helmet (opt), camping gear (or bring your own), wet bags, shuttle service, and meals.
