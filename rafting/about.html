Dry Oar River Rafting Company

About Us

The Dry Oar Rafting Company is located in Spanish Fork, Utah. We have a team of experienced river guides ready to take you on the ride of your life down the beautiful Bear River.

We offer half-day river trips, full-day trips, and week-long trips.

Dry Oar Rafting Company was started by Gordon and Julie Mathis out of a love for rafting and the beauty of nature.

Our guides have been certified in CPR, First Aid, and Advanced Rafting Skills. Each guide loves what they do, cares about their passengers, and seek to provide a memorable and safe journey.

About the River

The Bear River is located just 10 miles up Wild Rock Canyon from Spanish Fork, Utah, and provides the perfect blend of relaxation, beauty, and thrills. It has breathtaking scenery, interesting wild life, calm waters and exciting world class rapids. There are many grassy river banks for stopping to swim, fish, eat lunch, or camp for the night. Our guides will take you on the adventure of a lifetime!
